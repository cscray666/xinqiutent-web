Company Profile

Founded in 2011, Ningbo Xinqiu Tourism Products Co., Ltd. is a professional industry and trade manufacturer of outdoor tents. As an Alibaba Gold Supplier with BSCI certification, we offer stable supply, strict quality control, and flexible customization. We serve global buyers with reliable outdoor shelter solutions and efficient cross-border trade service.

  • Verified Supplier for Global Retailers (Carrefour, etc.)
  • Professional R&D Team with 30+ New Styles Yearly
  • Strict Quality Control covering every production line

Global Market Reach

Our outdoor tents are exported to over 80 countries & regions, with core markets in North America, Western Europe, Oceania, Southeast Asia and the Middle East. North America and Europe take the largest share, driven by high demand for camping, beach & privacy tents.

Fast growth is seen in Australia, UAE, South Korea, Malaysia and Brazil. We serve brand buyers, wholesalers, cross-border sellers and importers with stable bulk supply, OEM/ODM customization and on-time delivery.

Quality Assurance

At Ningbo Xinqiu, quality is our top priority. Supported by high ratings and positive feedback from customers, we implement strict raw material inspection, production control and finished product testing.

With BSCI certification and standardized manufacturing, we deliver durable, safe and reliable outdoor tents to earn long-term trust from global buyers.

Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is your minimum order quantity (MOQ)?

Our MOQ typically ranges from 500 to 1000 pieces per style for custom orders, depending on the material and structure.

2. Do you provide OEM and ODM services?

Yes, as a professional manufacturer, we offer full OEM/ODM customization, including logo printing, color selection, and structural redesign.

3. How long is your typical lead time?

Our average lead time is 30 to 45 days for mass production after sample approval and deposit receipt.

4. What quality certifications do you have?

We are a BSCI certified factory. Our products comply with various international standards including CE and REACH.
